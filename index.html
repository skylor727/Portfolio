About Me

Hi, I'm Skylor! A Software Engineer based out of Columbia, SC.

I’m originally from Phoenix, Arizona, and I’ve always been interested in technology. I began my professional career in the retail field, where I enjoyed all of the in-person interaction. However, as time passed, I realized that it was not my true passion. From there, I moved into the banking field, which I enjoyed as it offered a fast-paced, continuously challenging career. As time went on, I still felt the itch to move into a more technology-based field, as in my personal life I have always gravitated towards working with technology, whether it was building computers or learning about the inner workings of programs. I eventually decided to take the plunge and pursue my dream of becoming a software engineer.
